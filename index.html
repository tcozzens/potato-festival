Come join the fun at the 43rd Annual Mantua Potato Festival. This year's festival will feature amazing food, crafters' tent, local farmer's market, inflatables for the kids, Kiddie tractor pulls, Potato Stomp Race, cook off, beer & wine tent, 4 great bands, DJ & karaoke every night along with raffles, Chinese auction and contests galore. The weekend is jam packed full of fun and Sunday tops it all off with a lively parade down Main Street. Make sure to like us on Facebook and don't miss out on this year's Festival!
